Part Improvement

A fundamental crux of the ProQuorum system is the expression of the endolysin which is responsible for the actual lysis and consequent killing of the pathogenic C.difficile . Thus, there is a clear need for an endolysin that fulfills the criteria of being
    optimally expressed
    optimally secreted and
    optimally effective
      against C.difficile .

Thus, we redesigned the BBa_K895005 part submitted by the 2012 Dundee team 1 , which relied on:

    truncated form of the CD27L endolysin, shown by Mayer et al. (2011) 2 to increase both efficacy and host range
    Type VI secretion system derived from S. typhimurium

In contrast, our novel part BBa_…………. (CD27L_Assay) incorporated:

    full-length form of the CD27L endolysin 3

Incorporating both of these parts into pET28A, our expression vector, we planned on testing the relative efficacies of B.subtilis killing as per our killing assays.
