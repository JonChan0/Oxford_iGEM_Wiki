Experimentation

Our general pipeline for wet lab progress was as follows: !!!!!THIS SHOULD BE ILLUSTRATED IN A GRAPHIC!!!!

E.coli Pipeline:

  • PCR amplification of DNA constructs and plasmid backbones with appropriate primers to create overhangs for Gibson Assembly.
  • Gibson Assembly of our constructs into pET28A vector for transformation into E.coli.
  • Heat-shock transformation of assembled constructs into E.coli (NEBTurbo Competent E.coli) for cloning.
  • Miniprep of E.coli to obtain transformed DNA + Sequencing to confirm successful transformation.
  • Heat-shock transformation of sequenced DNA into E.coli (BL21 (DE3)-RIPL Competent E.coli) for gene expression.
  • Induction of gene expression with IPTG
  • Protein purification via Ni-NTA purifcation columns (for His tag) and/or SpyDock purifcation column (for SpyTag)

    • L. reuteri Pipeline:

  • PCR Amplification of DNA constructs and plasmid backbones with appropriate primers to create overhangs for Gibson Assembly.
  • Gibson Assembly of our constructs into pTRKH3 vector for transformation into L. reuteri.
  • Electroporation of assembled constructs into L. reuteri.
