Wet Lab Overview

Our progress in the wet lab has been a major component of our project. This page contains brief summaries and links to the different components of our wet lab work.

Experimentation

Our general pipeline for wet lab progress was as follows:

E.coli Pipeline:

  • PCR Amplification of DNA constructs and plasmid backbones with appropriate primers to create overhangs for Gibson Assembly.
  • Gibson Assembly of our constructs into pET28A vector for transformation into E.coli.
  • Heat-shock transformation of assembled constructs into E.coli.
